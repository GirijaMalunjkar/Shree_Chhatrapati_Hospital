About Us

Welcome to Shree Chhatrapati Hospital

Who We Are

Shree Chhatrapati Hospital, is situated in Dhule, Maharashtra, India. Established in April 2021 during covid time in view of serving patients. We specialize in a wide range of medical disciplines, including Neurosurgery & Trauma, Orthopaedic and Joint Replacement Surgery, Plastic Surgery, General Medicine, Nephrology, General Surgery, and many more specialty services. Our multidisciplinary approach ensures that our patients receive comprehensive care tailored to their specific needs. We prioritize patient safety and comfort, creating an environment conducive to healing and recovery. At Shree Chhatrapati Hospital, we leverage our expertise and resources to deliver accurate and efficient medical care.

Services And Specialization

Neurosurgery

The surgical management of disorders affecting the brain, spine, and nervous system.

Orthopaedic Surgery

Medical specialty focused on bones, joints & associated structures.

Joint Replacement Surgery

It treats joint conditions to improve mobility & reduce pain.

Plastic Surgery

Focuses on restoring lost function and improving self-esteem by altering appearance.

General Medicine

Medical specialty deals with blood pressure, diabetes, and lifestyle diseases.

Nephrology

Medical specialty focused on the diagnosis & treatment of kidney diseases.

General Surgery

Medical specialty deals with problems related to the chest and abdomen.

ICU

Critical care unit, saving lives with compassion and advanced technology.

What we Stand For

Our Premium Facilities

Dedicated Neuro Trauma Center

Experience exceptional care at our dedicated Neuro Trauma Center. Our specialized team of neurosurgeons, trauma surgeons, and neurologists provide comprehensive treatment for traumatic brain and spinal cord injuries. With state-of-the-art technology and a compassionate approach, we deliver timely and advanced care from stabilization to rehabilitation. Your well-being is our priority.

Well-equipped ICU

Experience exceptional care at our Well-equipped ICU Center. Our dedicated team of skilled healthcare professionals provides specialized care for critically ill patients using state-of-the-art technology. From advanced monitoring to life-saving interventions, we ensure the highest level of medical support. With a focus on patient safety and comfort, trust us for comprehensive critical care services.

Operation Theater

Our medical center is equipped with two advanced operation theaters (OT) designed to meet the highest standards of surgical care. Our OTs are equipped with state-of-the-art technology, ensuring a sterile and controlled environment for surgical procedures. With a dedicated team of skilled surgeons and support staff, we prioritize patient safety and precision during every operation.

Laboratory Service

There is a central collection center for receiving and collecting various specimens for testing. The timing for receiving specimens are at OPD- 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM & IPD-Round a clock. Also emergency labarotary services are available 24 hours for limited tests.

Dialysis Center

Our dialysis center provides comprehensive and compassionate care using advanced technology to enhance the lives of individuals with renal health needs. Our dedicated team prioritizes both physical and emotional well-being, creating a supportive community and offering personalized treatment plans, educational resources, and ongoing support for optimal health outcomes.

Radiology

Discover our cutting-edge CT Scan Center, providing advanced imaging for accurate diagnoses. Our skilled team of radiologists and technicians utilize state-of-the-art technology to deliver detailed cross-sectional images of your body. From routine screenings to complex diagnostics, we prioritize your safety and comfort. Trust us for timely and precise imaging to support your healthcare needs.

Pathology

Our pathology facility at our medical center is a leading-edge hub for comprehensive diagnostic services. Equipped with state-of-the-art technology and staffed by a team of expert pathologists, we are committed to delivering accurate and timely analysis of specimens to aid in the detection, diagnosis, and treatment planning of various diseases. Our advanced laboratory infrastructure enables us to conduct a wide range of tests,ensuring the highest quality of results.

Physiotherapy Center

Experience exceptional care at our Physiotherapy Center. Our skilled team of physiotherapists offers personalized treatment plans to help you regain strength and mobility. From manual therapy to cutting-edge techniques, we're committed to optimizing your recovery journey. Regain independence and enhance your quality of life with our comprehensive services.

All TPA's Cashless Facilities

Experience hassle-free cashless hospitalization with our All TPA's Cashless Facilities service. We have partnered with a wide network of TPAs to ensure a smooth insurance claims process. Our dedicated team will assist you in obtaining necessary approvals for cashless hospitalization, relieving you of financial concerns. Focus on your health while we provide convenient and stress-free access to quality healthcare with maximum insurance benefits.

24*7 Pharmacy

Access round-the-clock pharmaceutical services at our 24*7 Pharmacy. Our friendly pharmacists are available to assist with prescriptions, medication inquiries, and over-the-counter recommendations. With a wide range of products in stock, we provide prompt and reliable service for all your healthcare needs. Trust us for quality care and convenience, anytime

Emergency Service

Experience life-saving care with our Emergency Service. Our skilled team is equipped to handle any medical crisis, providing immediate assessment, stabilization, and treatment. With state-of-the-art facilities and a dedicated staff, we deliver prompt and effective care for sudden illnesses, severe injuries, and emergencies. Trust us to prioritize your well-being and provide compassionate support in critical moments.

100+ Beds Available

We have over 100 beds available, ensuring ample capacity for patient care. Our spacious and well-equipped facility provides a comfortable environment for a variety of medical conditions and treatments. From general care to specialized treatments and surgeries, our hospital can accommodate a larger number of patients while maintaining high standards of quality and personalized attention.

Nursing Staff

Experience compassionate and skilled care with our Nursing Staff service. Our highly trained nurses provide round-the-clock monitoring, medication administration, and support for patient well-being. Trust our dedicated team to create a nurturing environment where your needs are prioritized.
