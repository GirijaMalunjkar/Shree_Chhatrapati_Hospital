Our Introduction

Shree Chhatrapati Hospital

Your Journey to Better Health Starts Here!

Shree Chhatrapati Neurotrauma And Superspeciality Hospital established in April 2021 during covid time in view of serving patients and also considering a need of superficiality services with respect to Neurosurgery & Trauma, Orthopaedic Surgery, Joint Replacement Surgery, Plastic Surgery, General Medicine, Nephrology, General Surgery and many more specialty services to provide comprehensive care. At Shree Chhatrapati Hospital, We strive to create an environment where patients feel supported, comfortable, and confident in their treatment journey. Our state-of-the-art facilities and advanced medical technologies enable us to provide cutting-edge treatments and procedures, ensuring the best possible outcomes for our patients.

Services And Specialization

Neurosurgery

Orthopaedic Surgery

Medical specialty focused on bones, joints & associated structures.

Joint Replacement Surgery

It treats joint conditions to improve mobility & reduce pain.

Plastic Surgery

General Medicine

Medical specialty deals with blood pressure, diabetes, and lifestyle diseases.

Nephrology

Medical specialty focused on the diagnosis & treatment of kidney diseases.

ICU
